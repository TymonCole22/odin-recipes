Lasagna

Description

Indulge in the savory layers of a classic lasagna with this delectable recipe. Begin by crafting a rich meat sauce, marrying ground beef with aromatic garlic, onions, and tomatoes, simmered to perfection. Meanwhile, prepare a velvety bechamel sauce, infusing mile with nutmeg for a creamy undertone. Embrace the art of layering as you assemble the lasagna: start with a generous spread of a meat sauce, followed by sheets of perfectly cooked lasagna noodles, and a luscious coating of bechamel. Sprinkle a blend of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese to add a delightful gooey texture and irresistible golden crust. Repeat the layers until your baking dish is a symphony of flavors and textures. Finally, bake to golden perfection, allowing the aromas to fill your kitchen and your taste buds to revel in the hearty, conforting embrace of this timeless Italian masterpiece.

Ingredients

  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Beef
  • Cheese
  • Pasta Sauce

Steps

  1. Boil pasta: Cook in a large pot of salted water per the recipe below.
  2. Prepare meat sauce: Cook sausage and beef with onion and garlic. Drain well, add the pasta sauce & simmer it for a few minutes to thicken.
  3. Combine cheese mixture: Stir the cheese mixture together in a bowl.
  4. Layer & bake: Layer the meat sauce and cheese mixture with lasagna noodles and bake until browned and bubbly.
